Who We Are


Walden is proud to have an online learning community that is as distinctive as it is diverse. Walden practices a policy of nondiscrimination in admission to, access to, and employment in its online degree programs and activities. Walden does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, sex, age, religion or creed, marital status, disability, national or ethnic origin, socioeconomic status, sexual orientation, or other legally protected status.


Our online learning community is made up of students and faculty, alumni and friends, national experts and visiting scholars, and a growing list of academic, corporate, and government partners who share our commitment to transforming our world through higher education.


Our Values


MISSION: Walden University provides a diverse community of career professionals with the opportunity to transform themselves as scholar-practitioners so that they can effect positive social change.


VISION: Walden University envisions a distinctively different 21st-century learning community where knowledge is judged worthy to the degree that it can be applied by its graduates to the immediate solutions of critical societal challenges, thereby advancing the greater global good.

Degrees

What we offer


Undergraduate

Our online bachelor’s degree programs combine academic study with practical skills you can use immediately to grow in your field.

Graduate

There’s a future that’s yours for the taking. Whether you’re looking to move into leadership or embark on a new career, Walden’s master’s programs allow you to focus your studies in an area that directly impacts your goals.

Doctoral

Walden’s doctoral programs provide a path for lifelong professional and personal achievement.



Certificates

A Walden certificate can provide you a quick, cost-effective option for building your skills and knowledge. Whether you're looking to advance in your current organization or switch industries, our certificate programs can give you the edge you need to choose your own path to success.

Accreditation

Accreditation is a mark of quality. Walden has been accredited by the Higher Learning Commission (HLC) since 1990. Being accredited by the HLC validates that our institution lives its mission, operates with integrity, and provides the resources and support for our students to succeed.

Social Change

Walden is focused on inspiring and celebrating the remarkable achievements of our diverse community members who continue to make a difference by addressing challenges where they live, in their professions, and in the world around them..
